Here's How It Works.

  1. 1. Select Your Celebrity's Photo

    Your star hand-writes a personal message on every egraph, which is why each one starts with a hand selected, professional grade photo. Think of it like the poster or polaroid that you stood in line to get autographed years ago.

  2. 2. Write Your Star a Note

    Everyone loves to be loved, and your star is no different. Remind him of your chance encounter at the supermarket. Ask him to wish you a happy birthday. Reveal your secret crush. He will personally read every single word.

    What can I write? »

  3. 3. Your Star Responds into the Mic

    Believe it or not, we book a block of your celebrity's time with your name on it, moments after you finish your note. This gives him plenty of time to find a quiet spot, hand-write you a response, and speak a short message just for you into his iPad's microphone.

    How do I know it's really him? »

It's Your Egraph – Ready to Be Shared!

Your interaction with your star is a rare and unique experience that deserves to be enjoyed and shared. As soon as your celebrity finishes speaking, we capture his voice and script, making them available to you forever, timelessly, in a one-of-a-kind egraph.
