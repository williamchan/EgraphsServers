Careers

Egraphs was founded in September 2011 with the goal of connecting stars and their fans on a deeper, more personal level. In July 2012, we launched www.egraphs.com and our first product, an egraph. It's a shared moment between a star and fan that's captured forever - think of it as a next-generation autograph that includes both a personal handwritten note and voice message from the star to the fan. Egraphs are authenticated, shareable, enduring, and currently available from over 130 Major League Baseball players. We seek to change the way fans connect with their stars by building products with global potential. You can read about our launch in this ESPN article.

Developer Operations Engineer - Seattle, WA

We seek an experienced developer operations engineer to help us deploy, monitor, and administer our growing footprint of servers and critical services, as well as improve overall site performance. This is a chance to be one of the founding members of the Egraphs DevOps team. You will be working closely with a team of software engineers who believe in rational and transparent decisionmaking. Egos play no role here... humor plays a rather large role.
