What is an egraph?

An egraph is a personalized interaction between a star and fan. The fan receives a 100% personalized and authenticated written and audio message – along with a handpicked high quality image – in a beautiful digital gallery that can be shared with the world. The egraph includes:

The Biometric Authentication Process

The first thing we do with any star is personally sit down with him to create his biometric footprint – a two-factor identification of his voice and signature profile. By doing this, we know that it’s him creating egraphs for fans, no matter where he is in the world. Getting a great biometric footprint used in verification is just important for us as it is to you and the star because we’re obsessed about the quality and authenticity of the egraph you receive. Here’s how we create the biometric footprint:

Voiceprint

We collect audio samples from each star to create his voiceprint. Our technology extracts pitch, tone , cadence and other voice patterns that get turned into mathematical representations/statistical models. We store these representations in electronic format for use in authentication.

Written Signature Profile

Like the voiceprint, we collect in-person samples of the star’s signature. Our signature identification technology measures a person’s speed, rhythm, acceleration and movement when they sign their name on an iPad. Similar to the voiceprint, these are stored as complex algorithms for use in authentication.

Authentication: When a celebrity creates an egraph, we test both the audio message and the written signature to verify it’s your star that actually created it. If the voice message and signature pass our tests, then the egraph immediately gets sent to you. If the voice message recording and the signature don’t match with the voiceprint and signature profile we captured during enrollment, we have someone from our Quality Assurance team look into the issue. If we’re not sure it’s your star, then we won’t pass the egraph to you. We will let you know immediately and refund your money while we look further into the matter. That is our commitment to you – because we cannot afford to lose your trust in the quality and authenticity of the product.

What Can I Do With My Egraphs?

Receiving an email directly from your star with a link to your egraph is just the beginning. Here are all of the things that you can do:

  1. Check out the egraph in its entirety – the image, written message and audio recording. Plus, you can view the certificate of authenticity for all of the vital details about when the egraph was created.
  2. Share it with your friends and family via your favorite social network. Try to tweet it back to your star! You never know what they’ll say and do.
  3. Download the egraph – the image and written signature – in a high resolution file. Set is as your computer wallpaper or load it onto a digital picture frame!
  4. Purchase a high quality framed print – maybe you want to hang it up on your wall. We offer a service that enables you to send a beautiful, framed egraph – with the certificate of authenticity – anywhere in the world.
  5. Send it as a greeting card – let’s say you’re giving dad an egraph for his birthday; you can also give him an egraph greeting card (similar in size and shape to a birthday card) that contains the egraph image and written signature with a personalized message from you. Plus, the card has the certificate of authenticity and a QR code that links directly to the star’s voice message online.
