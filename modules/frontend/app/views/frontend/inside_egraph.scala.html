Inside an egraph

This page takes you inside the Egraphs product and technology.

  1. The components of an egraph
  2. The biometric authentication process
  3. Enjoying and sharing your egraph

What is an egraph?

An egraph is a shared moment between you and your favorite star. It is immortalized forever with a beautiful high resolution photograph, handwritten note, signature, and personalized voice message. It's his voice, his handwriting, his time — every egraph is one-of-a-kind.

The biometric authentication process

The first thing we do with any star is personally sit down with them to create a biometric fingerprint – a two-factor identification of their voice and signature profile. By doing this, we can verify that it’s that person creating egraphs for fans, no matter where they are in the world.

Getting a thorough biometric fingerprint used in verification is of the upmost importance to us. It’s the basis of the trust both fans and stars have in the authenticity of egraphs.

Here’s how we create the biometric fingerprint:

Voiceprint

We collect audio samples from each star to create his voiceprint. Our technology extracts pitch, tone, cadence and other voice patterns that get turned into mathematical representations and statistical models. We store these representations in electronic format for use in authentication (see below).

Written signature profile

Like the voiceprint, we collect in-person samples of the star’s signature. Our signature identification technology measures a person’s speed, rhythm, acceleration and movement when they sign their name on an iPad. Similar to the voiceprint, these are stored as complex algorithms for use in authentication (see below).

Authentication: When a star creates an egraph, we examine both the audio message and the written signature. We match them to the star's biometric fingerprint in order to verify the creator of the egraph. After the voice message and signature pass our tests, the egraph immediately gets sent to the fan. Rest assured we will not deliver any egraphs unless we're completely sure the star created the egraph.

Enjoying and sharing your egraph

Receiving an email directly from a player with a link to the egraph is just the beginning. Here are all of the things that fans can do with their egraphs:

  1. Check out the egraph in the digital gallery – the image, written message and audio recording. Plus, you can view the authenticity details from when the egraph was created.
  2. Share it with friends and family via your favorite social network. You can even tweet it back to the star and see what their reaction is. You never know what they’ll say and do.
  3. Purchase a high quality framed print. We offer a service that enables fans to order a beautiful, framed egraph – with the certificate of authenticity – to be shipped anywhere in the country.
