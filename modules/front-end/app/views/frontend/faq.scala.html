Frequently Asked Questions

What is an egraph?

An egraph is a shared moment between you and your star, captured forever. It is the next generation autograph -- no traditional pen & paper involved. It starts with you writing a message to the star. Your star will personally read this message on an iPad. Then, based on what you requested and the information you provide for your egraph, your star will create an authenticated written and audio message just for you using a special iPad app. No two egraphs are alike. Each one looks and sounds different—your egraph was created just for you (or your gift recipient) based on your original message to the star. Egraphs can be viewed online on your computer, smartphone, or tablet device, or you can have them printed and framed to display on a wall or desk.

How does Egraphs bring the stars to you?

It begins when stars tell us they want to create egraphs for their fans. To begin, we make sure they are set up with an iPad and the Egraphs iPad app. It’s through the technology of the iPad that we’re able to bring you any star from anywhere in the world at any time. We enroll the stars in person, gathering voiceprints and handwriting samples. These are used later on to verify that the star you contacted is the one writing and recording an egraph for you. No middle men. No generic signed photographs. Each egraph is unique and personal, created individually for you.

How exactly does a star create an egraph?

Once you order an egraph, your request is routed to the Egraphs iPad app on your star’s iPad. When the star has some time to connect with their fans, they will open up the Egraphs app. The first thing they see is your message. That’s important – this lets them know who you are and why you’re a fan. Then the star goes on to create the personalized written and voice message to you through their iPad. They use an iPad stylus to hand-write the messages, and they use the iPad’s microphone to record the audio message.

What message can I send a star?

Anything! Well ... just about anything. You can tell the star how big a fan you are. Or that you went to the same high school. Or that you’re going to see him play when he comes to your town. It’s up to you. The only thing we ask is that your message is respectful to the star, just as we expect the star to be respectful in the written and audio messages they create for you. If your message doesn’t pass, we’ll kick it back to you to revise before we send it on to the star. For the full Terms of Use, click here.

How many egraphs are available from my star? What happens if they sell out?

Each star agrees to create a certain number of egraphs in a specified period of time. Because each egraph is created individually, they can’t be rushed or mass-produced. That’s why a star will only agree to create a certain number at a time. They want to do it right. This can result in egraphs from a certain star selling out temporarily. When that happens, you’ll need to wait until the star decides to sign another batch. The celebrities set their own schedules for when and how many egraphs they sign.

If your favorite star’s egraphs are sold out, send an email to requests@@egraphs.com with your star's name and we'll let you know when they become available, or follow @@Egraphs on Twitter and we'll announce it there.

How long will it be before I get my egraph?

Stars agree to create egraphs for fans in a timely manner. When you order your egraph, you will be given a date by which you can expect your egraph to be delivered. The delivery date is conservative but still not a guarantee. Over 50% of orders will be delivered in half the time of the expected date. Framed prints typically arrive 3 weeks after the digital egraph has been delivered. If you have a specific need to get something ahead of time, send us an email to support@@egraphs.com and we'll try to grease the wheels. If we foresee any issues with a star finishing your egraph by this date, we will let you know as soon as possible.

Can I give an egraph as a gift to someone else?

Yes, an egraph makes a great gift for a friend or family member. When ordering your egraph, be sure that you select ‘Yes’ where it asks “Is this a gift?” You’ll then be able to enter in the name and email address of the gift recipient. When the star creates the egraph, the recipient will receive an email with information on how to view the completed egraph. If you want the recipient to receive the egraph on a certain date (such as a birthday, anniversary, or special holiday) please email us at support@@egraphs.com with your order number and the date you’d like it delivered. Instead of delivering the egraph as soon as the star creates it, we’ll wait until the date you specify so the recipient gets a nice surprise in their email on the special day.

I can't view or play my egraph, or I see a message about an SVGZ file. What should I do?

Egraphs can be viewed and heard using nearly any modern browser. Some older browsers don't automatically support the technology needed to view/hear egraphs successfully. Good browsers to use include: FireFox, Chrome, Internet Explorer 9, or Safari.

The technology a browser needs to support to view the egraph is SVGZ (an Adobe file format) and for hearing the audio it is HTML5 or Flash (Adobe again). If your browser does not support one of these, you should be able to find a plugin that will. If you are using Internet Explorer 7 or 8, you will need to either upgrade to IE 9, or install a plugin to allow the older browser to handle the Egraph page correctly. One such plugin can be found at http://www.google.com/chromeframe.

If you continue to have trouble viewing or hearing your egraph, please reach out to us at support@@egraphs.com.

The site terms say you have to be 13 to use the site. So can I give a gift to a child?

Assuming you have the parent's permission, you can submit the egraph order with the name of the child and the email address of the parent. In this case the parent will own the egraph and manage the privacy. Egraphs will transfer ownership of the egraph free of charge when the child is old enough to use the site.

Can I order an egraph or send one to someone who isn’t in the United States?

An egraph is sent via email, so an egraph can be ordered and sent to anyone anywhere in the world. The additional option of a framed print is currently only available to ship to the US and Canada. When placing an order for a framed print, if you can't put in a Canadian postal code, just put in 0000 and follow up with an email to support@@egraphs.com with the correct address.

How much is an egraph?

The price for an egraph depends on which star you’re ordering an egraph from. Some may cost $25, some may be $100 or more. To find the price for a star you’re interested in, find where they are listed on our home page and click on their picture. You’ll then see the prices listed below each available photo for that star. Please note that additional options, such as ordering a framed print of your egraph are in addition to the cost of the egraph.

Can I have more information about the framed print option?

The cost for a framed print of an egraph is $45, including a matte, frame, and certificate of authenticity. You can get an idea of what it looks like from this picture. The audio message does not play directly from the frame, but there is a QR code that can be scanned with a smart phone that would then take you to the digital version of the egraph so it could be played. When ordering your egraph, you have the option of selecting this option for $45, and you will also be asked for a shipping address. If you decide you’d like to order a framed print after your original purchase, just reach out to us at support@@egraphs.com and we’ll get your framed print order processed.
